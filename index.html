About The University

Mindanao State University - Maigo College of Education, Science, and Technology is a Diverse-Cultured college institution located at the heart of Lanao Del Norte. Providing top quality education to students in Northern Mindanao. Offering 20 College Programs such as BS Computer Science, BS Hospitality management, BS Social Work, and many more. Producing Top Notchers in the Licensure Examinations for Teachers (LET), in 2022, 2024, and 2025. MSU-MCEST surely provides quality education and unique college experince, having multiple cultures, religions, and ideologies united in one school with shared goals.
